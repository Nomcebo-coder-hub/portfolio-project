I thoroughly enjoyed the course because after each lesson there was a mini assignment to complete which further helped to solidify my understanding of the lesson. I went on to complete the Power Learn Project Software Development Programme. In this programme I learned courses in Web development; Python; Dart with Flutter; Software Engineering; Database and Entreprenuership and Personal Development.
